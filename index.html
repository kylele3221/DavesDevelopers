Description

The WISE Full-Depth Ecliptic Polar Atlas Image Sets are image data sets that make up the all sky atlas, a project that images the entire night sky. 41 Atlas Image sets make up the all sky atlas, and for our project we focused on the sonification of one of them; although since our processes are automated we could in theory output sonifications for all 41 tiles of the night sky.
